Get Proof of Employment End

You need official proof from your employer that the employment relationship is ending. This document is your key to changing your insurance status.

  • Request a formal termination letter (`Kündigung`) or a contract termination agreement (`Aufhebungsvertrag`).
  • It must clearly state the last day of employment. Since the business is closing, this should be straightforward.
  • A written statement from him confirming the business closure and the end of your employment on a specific date is essential.

3

Attempt to Cancel AOK Membership

Once you have proof the job ended, immediately inform AOK. Your goal is to get out of the "voluntary" membership before it really starts.

  • Provide AOK with the proof of employment ending.
  • State that you are a student in a preparatory course and wish to return to private insurance, as you are no longer eligible for mandatory public insurance.
  • There is a special, short-term right to cancel when mandatory insurance ends. Act within two weeks of receiving confirmation from AOK about the status change.
✓
4

Re-apply for Private Insurance

As soon as you have confirmation from AOK that your membership will end, secure new private insurance. Do NOT have a gap in coverage.

  • Contact Care Concept (or another private insurer for students like Mawista, HanseMerkur, etc.).
  • Explain your situation: you were a member, had a short-term job, and now need to resume your student insurance.
  • You will need your FSP enrollment certificate (`Immatrikulationsbescheinigung`).
  • Get the insurance confirmation document (`Versicherungsnachweis`) needed for the residence permit.
✓
5

Apply for Your Residence Permit

With proof of health insurance, you can proceed with your residence permit application at the Foreigners' Office (`Ausländerbehörde`) in Magdeburg.

  • Book an appointment immediately. Do not wait.
  • Prepare all required documents: passport, application form, biometric photo, proof of finance, FSP enrollment, rental contract, and your new health insurance certificate.
  • Go to your appointment and submit your application. Your valid application will secure your legal status even if the visa expires while they process it.

Why Did This Happen? Understanding German Law

The German social security system can be confusing. This situation wasn't anyone's fault, but rather the result of specific rules being automatically applied. Understanding these rules can help reduce confusion and stress.

Mandatory Public Insurance Rule

In Germany, if you have a job that pays above a certain threshold (a "Minijob" limit of €538/month), you are automatically required to have public health insurance. Your 20-hour/week contract likely put you over this limit. Your employer was legally obligated to register you.

The "Continuation" Rule

Once you are in the public system, you generally stay in. When the reason for your *mandatory* insurance (the job) disappears, the system doesn't kick you out. Instead, it automatically converts your membership to a *voluntary* one (`freiwillige Versicherung`) to ensure you don't become uninsured. This is why AOK will continue to bill you, but at a higher, non-employee rate.
